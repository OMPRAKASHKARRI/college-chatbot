EduReach College Complete Information

ABOUT EDUReach College:
EduReach College is a top engineering college located in Hyderabad, Telangana, India. It was established in 2005. The college is affiliated with JNTU Hyderabad and approved by AICTE.

The campus has smart classrooms, laboratories, library with 50,000 books, sports complex, and hostel facilities. The college has more than 150 faculty members and a student-to-faculty ratio of 15:1.
